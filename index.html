Who we are

DotQ was founded on the premise of bringing intrepid and enthusiastic researchers together for the sake of QC research. Born out of the Qiskit Global Summer School 2023, our group consists of 50 members from all around the world and from various different stages of higher education. We strive to foster global citizenship and international cooperation through the research that we produce.

Our goals

At DotQ, we hope to continue to grow our diverse community of researchers. We want to be able to provide research opportunities for all our members, from the most beginner to the most advanced. We hope to do this by establishing meaningful connections with other students, professors, mentors, research groups, non-profits, etc. that can provide the projects we need, and foster collaboration and education in the field of quantum computing.
